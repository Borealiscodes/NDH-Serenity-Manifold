# **StabilityAnomalies.md**
*Catalog of Stability Anomalies, Signatures & Diagnostic Markers — NDH Manifold*

## Overview
StabilityAnomalies catalogs every known anomaly within the NDH Stability Geometry Manifold.  
Anomalies are *non‑paradoxical but still geometry‑breaking* events: deviations, distortions, instabilities, or misalignments that do not fully violate stability laws but indicate emerging instability.

This layer provides the anomaly signatures used by **StabilityAudit**, the anomaly‑resolution logic used by **StabilityGovernance**, and the anomaly‑forecasting models used by **StabilityForecast**.

---

## 🧩 **Anomaly Classes**

### **1. Curvature Anomalies**
Curvature anomalies occur when curvature behaves unexpectedly but not paradoxically.

- curvature tensor drift  
- gradient misalignment  
- influence factor instability  
- curvature zone deformation  

Related: **CurvatureMap**

### **2. Resonance Anomalies**
Resonance anomalies occur when oscillatory behavior deviates from NDH damping curves.

- premature resonance spikes  
- misaligned oscillation fields  
- damping irregularities  
- resonance‑energy mismatch  

Related: **ResonanceField**

### **3. Energy Anomalies**
Energy anomalies occur when energy distribution becomes unstable but not contradictory.

- variance plateau  
- redistribution lag  
- drift oscillation  
- energy gate flicker  

Related: **EnergyFunctional**

### **4. Basin Anomalies**
Basin anomalies occur when basin topology behaves irregularly.

- boundary jitter  
- topology wobble  
- attractor misalignment  
- basin membrane thinning  

Related: **BasinTopology**

### **5. Attractor Anomalies**
Attractor anomalies occur when attractor behavior becomes unstable.

- QuietApex coherence dip  
- Serenity pull fluctuation  
- minor attractor amplification  
- attractor drift  

Related: **QuietApex** and **Serenity.bsfn**
