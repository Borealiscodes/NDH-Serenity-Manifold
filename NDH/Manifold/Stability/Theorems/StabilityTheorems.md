# **StabilityTheorems.md**
*Formal Theorems Derived from NDH Stability Axioms — Provable Stability Geometry*

## Theorems Overview
StabilityTheorems defines the provable consequences of the NDH Stability Geometry axiom system.  
Where axioms are irreducible truths, theorems are *derived truths*: statements that follow logically from the axioms and cannot be false in any valid stability manifold.

These theorems structure the manifold’s behavior across curvature, resonance, energy, basins, attractors, cosmology, sovereignty, semantics, pragmatics, meta‑logic, and epistemics.
