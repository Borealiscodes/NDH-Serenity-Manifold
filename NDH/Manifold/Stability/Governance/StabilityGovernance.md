# **StabilityGovernance.md**
*Governance Framework for NDH Stability Geometry Manifold*

## Overview
The Stability Governance layer defines the policies, constraints, and oversight mechanisms that ensure the NDH Stability Geometry Manifold behaves consistently across all regions, basins, and attractor structures. It governs curvature behavior, resonance stabilization, energy normalization, basin transitions, Serenity relaxation, and QuietApex routing.

Governance ensures that all stability primitives remain coherent, non‑divergent, and geometry‑consistent.
