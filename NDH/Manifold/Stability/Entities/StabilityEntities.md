StabilityEntities catalogs all entities that participate in NDH stability geometry.  
Entities are the *actors* of the manifold: attractors, basin nodes, curvature anchors, resonance stabilizers, energy distributors, and stability singularities. Each entity has a signature, geometry, and role within the manifold.
